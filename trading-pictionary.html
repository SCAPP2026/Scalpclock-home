Why a Pictionary Instead of a Word List

Trading vocabulary is easy to read and hard to retain — "support" and "resistance" mean nothing until you've actually seen price react at a level. Pairing each definition with a small diagram closes that gap faster than text alone, the same way a chart makes a setup click in a way a paragraph never does.

This covers the core price-action vocabulary you'll hear in any Learn Hub lesson, plus the options-specific terms — strikes, Greeks, ITM/OTM — that trip up traders moving from stock to options trading.
