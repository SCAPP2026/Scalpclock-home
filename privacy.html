Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 30, 2026  ·  Last Updated: June 30, 2026  ·  VSLLC

Short version: We collect your email address to run your account. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. You can request deletion at any time.

1. Who We Are

ScalpClock is owned and operated by VSLLC ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use ScalpClock at scalpclock.com, including all associated tools (ScalpCharts, ScalpExit, Trade Journal).

Questions? Email us at support@scalpclock.com

2. Information We Collect

Category What We Collect Why
Account Data Email address, encrypted password Create and manage your account
Trade Journal Data Trades you log: symbol, direction, entry/exit price, P&L, notes Power your personal trade journal
Payment Data Subscription status, billing period (no card numbers) Manage your subscription tier
Usage Data Pages visited, features used, ticker lookups, session duration Improve the platform and fix bugs
Device / Technical Data Browser type, operating system, IP address, time zone Security, fraud prevention, compatibility
Local Storage App state saved in your browser (e.g., saved option contract, entry price) Restore your session across page reloads — stays on your device only

We do not collect Social Security numbers, government IDs, brokerage account credentials, or any biometric data.

3. How We Collect Information

  • Directly from you — when you create an account, log a trade, or contact us
  • Automatically — via standard web server logs and your browser when you use the platform
  • From third parties — Stripe sends us subscription status updates when you pay or cancel

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Deliver the ScalpClock service, including signals, charts, and coaching tools
  • Process payments and manage your subscription through Stripe
  • Store and display your personal trade journal entries
  • Send transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset, billing receipts)
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access
  • Improve the platform based on how features are used
  • Respond to support requests and inquiries

We do not use your data to serve third-party advertisements. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your account data and trade journal are stored securely in Supabase, a cloud database platform with encrypted storage at rest and in transit (TLS). Supabase infrastructure is hosted in the United States.

Payment processing is handled exclusively by Stripe. VSLLC never stores your credit card number or full payment details on our servers.

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage method is 100% secure. You use the Service at your own risk, and we encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

7. Cookies and Local Storage

ScalpClock uses browser local storage (not traditional cookies) to save your app state between sessions — for example, remembering your last tracked option contract, ticker selection, or entry price. This data is stored entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

We may use standard browser cookies for authentication session management (to keep you logged in). These are essential to the Service and cannot be disabled without logging out.

We do not use tracking cookies, ad pixels, or third-party analytics cookies.

8. Data Retention

We retain your account information and trade journal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data from our active databases within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law (e.g., financial transaction records).

Backups may retain data for an additional period before being purged from our backup systems.

9. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your account and associated personal data
  • Portability: Request your trade journal data in a portable format
  • Opt-out of emails: Unsubscribe from non-transactional emails at any time via the link in any email we send

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@scalpclock.com with your request. We will respond within 30 days.

10. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete your personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information.

We do not sell your personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us at support@scalpclock.com.

11. European Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance — to provide the Service you signed up for
  • Legitimate interests — to improve the platform, prevent fraud, and ensure security
  • Legal obligation — to comply with applicable laws

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe we have not complied with applicable data protection laws.

12. Children's Privacy

ScalpClock is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a minor, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@scalpclock.com.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. For material changes, we will notify you by email at your registered address. Your continued use of ScalpClock after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
