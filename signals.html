What are options and stock signals?
ScalpClock's signals scan the market's most active stocks for RSI, VWAP, and volume conditions that historically precede a directional move, then flag each ticker as a potential Calls (bullish) or Puts (bearish) setup.
How often do signals update?
Signals refresh continuously throughout market hours, scanning 100+ of the most active tickers using live RSI(14), VWAP distance, and volume-surge data.
Are these signals financial advice?
No. ScalpClock's signals are educational only — not financial advice. Options trading carries substantial risk of loss, and past signal performance does not guarantee future results.
