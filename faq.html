The Basics

ScalpClock provides real market signals — but you execute all trades yourself through your own brokerage. We are a signal and timing platform, not a broker. There is no paper trading mode; every signal reflects live or recent market data. Your money, your decisions, your broker.

The live signal board refreshes every 60 seconds during market hours. Each refresh pulls the latest 5-minute bars from our data feed, recalculates RSI(14), VWAP, and volume surge for all 20 tickers, and re-ranks the top 10 opportunities. Signal quality is better when the market is actively trading (9:45 AM–4:00 PM ET).

A HARD signal is our highest-confidence alert. It fires when multiple indicators align simultaneously: RSI is in extreme territory (≤20 oversold or ≥80 overbought), price is on the wrong side of VWAP, and volume is surging above the 20-bar average. Seeing HARD on a call or put means the statistical edge is stacked — it's not just one indicator, it's confluence. Learn more about HARD signals →

The live signal board on the home page is accessible without an account. To use the Exit Assistant, trade journal, or dashboard, you need a free ScalpClock account. Creating one takes under 30 seconds — just an email and password, no credit card required.
